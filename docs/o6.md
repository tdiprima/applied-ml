## What is Isolation Forest? 🏝️🌳

Isolation Forest is a **super-smart tree gang** that finds **weirdos** (anomalies) in your data.  

- Normal data: Lots of similar points, hard to split. 😴  
- Weirdos (anomalies): Few & different, easy to "isolate" alone fast! ⚡  

It builds **random trees** (like a forest) that **slice data paths** until weirdos are cornered quick.  
Emoji memory: 🏝️ = Isolate loners. 🌳🌳 = Forest of decision trees.  

### Why Perfect for Detecting System Freakouts? 🚨🛡️

**Use case**: Spot **unusual system behavior** BEFORE crashes/logs explode. Like a **guard dog** barking early!
